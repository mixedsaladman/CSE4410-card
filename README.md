Developers: Giovanni Almaraz, Jeff Lee, David Nguyen

Description: The name of the game is Scoundrel. A dungeon crawler card game, where you fight through the Club/Spade suits and manage your health and resources, the Heart suit healing you and the Diamond suit reducing the damage you take. Choose carefully, whether you decide to avoid the fight completely, having to confront it later, or face them now, arranging them to minimize the damage you take.
